Well, here are some things about me:

Geek

I have been in front of a computer since I was just four, and since then, I just simply could not stop! It was like my place where I could just be myself, create, and do all the things that I love. I've been always interested in technology in general; from something so basic as a simple little bit, to something so big and complex as an, I don't know, an Artificial Intelligence with a deep neural network to make cars full self-driving. Things like that drives me crazy! And precisely by things like that, it's why I just simply love being a programmer <3, and have planned to learn and apply everything as I can from this world.

Big Dreamer

Bill Gates, Elon Musk, Linus Torvalds... Those people are just great references for me, and helped me to reach where I'm actually are. Trying always to perform, to push myself, to learn something new... And with that, at the end of the day, be able to create. To contribute.
